Public Product Website

SmartShop AI

SmartShop AI helps users discover better buying opportunities by tracking price movement, classifying deal quality, and suggesting when to buy now versus wait.

Direct-Link First

Recommendations prioritize direct store links so the platform works even without affiliate onboarding.

Deal Signal Engine

Deals are labeled with transparent logic such as Genuine Bargain, Fake Discount, or Normal.

Multi-Market Tracking

Price data is collected across major marketplaces to compare trend and availability.

How This Service Is Used

This website represents the SmartShop AI recommendation service where users can view product insights, price history context, and outbound product links.

  • Users can browse recommendations generated from tracked products.
  • Users can click outbound product links to view items on marketplace websites.
  • Selected links may include referral parameters when available.

Affiliate Disclosure

Some outbound links on SmartShop AI may be affiliate links. If a qualifying purchase is made, SmartShop AI may receive a commission at no extra cost to the user.

This does not change product pricing shown to users, and recommendations are based on deal analysis, not commission priority.

Privacy Summary

  • We use minimal account and usage data to generate recommendations and alerts.
  • We do not sell personal data.
  • Users can request data deletion by contacting us.
